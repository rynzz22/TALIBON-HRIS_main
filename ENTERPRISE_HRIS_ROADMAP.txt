================================================================================
ENTERPRISE HRIS: 1.5 MONTH STRATEGIC DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP (6 WEEKS)
Municipality of Talibon - Human Resource Intelligence System (HRIS)
================================================================================

Total Duration: 45 Days (approx. 6 Weeks)
Objective: From MVP to a fully audited, production-ready Enterprise HRIS.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEEK 1: CORE ARCHITECTURE & SECURE FOUNDATION (Days 1-7)
Focus: Data Integrity & Database Hardening
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Day 1-2: Relational Data Modeling (Supabase/PostgreSQL)
    - Finalize 'employees', 'departments', 'positions' schemas.
    - Implement RLS (Row Level Security) to prevent cross-tenant data leaks.
[ ] Day 3-4: Authentication & Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
    - Setup Google Auth & Custom Claims (Admin, DeptHead, Employee, Payroll).
    - Build the Login Terminal with session persistence.
[ ] Day 5-7: Personnel Registry MVP
    - CRUD operations for the employee master list.
    - Integration of basic ID generation logic (PH-TAL-XXXX).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEEK 2: PERSONNEL INTELLIGENCE & GOV-COMPLIANCE (Days 8-14)
Focus: Rich Profiles & Statutory Tracking
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Day 8-10: Advanced Employee Profiles
    - Family background, Educational background, Service records (201 Files).
    - Digital Document Vault (File uploads for PDS, Birth Certs, etc.)
[ ] Day 11-12: Statutory Information Management
    - SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, TIN trackers.
    - Automated validity checks for government IDs.
[ ] Day 13-14: Departmental Hierarchy Mapping
    - Organizational structure visualization.
    - Department transfer history logs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEEK 3: ATTENDANCE TERMINAL & BIOMETRIC SYNC (Days 15-21)
Focus: Real-time Duty Monitoring
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Day 15-17: The "Smart Terminal" UI/UX
    - Precise Time-In/Out logic with GPS/Geofencing (Optional for field workers).
    - QR Code scanning for instant walk-in attendance.
[ ] Day 18-19: Biometric Hardware Integration (API Logic)
    - Architecting the bridge to ZKTeco/HikVision biometric data pushes.
    - Automated syncing of daily logs from devices to cloud.
[ ] Day 20-21: Exception Handling & Corrections
    - Workflow for "Missed Log" requests.
    - Department head approval gate for log corrections.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEEK 4: PAYROLL ENGINE & FISCAL GENERATIVE MATRICES (Days 22-28)
Focus: The Financial Core
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Day 22-24: Disbursement Engine (The Math)
    - Automated 13th-month, overtime, Hazard Pay, and night differential logic.
    - Dynamic deduction calculators (Updated Phic/SSS tables).
[ ] Day 25-26: The Generative Matrix (Master Ledger)
    - Export tools for PDF/Excel (Kensena 1 & 2).
    - Digital signature integration for official payroll sheets.
[ ] Day 27-28: Payroll History & Archiving
    - Snapshotting fiscal records for permanent municipal audit records.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEEK 5: LEAVE MOBILITY & EMPLOYEE SELF-SERVICE (ESS) (Days 29-35)
Focus: Productivity & Empowerment
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Day 29-31: Leave Management System (LMS)
    - Automated Leave Credit earn-rates (1.25/month).
    - Multi-stage approval worklow (Dept Head -> HR -> Mayor).
[ ] Day 32-33: Personnel Inquiry Desk (The Employee Portal)
    - Mobile-responsive view for Payslips and Personal Attendance logs.
    - Personal Announcement Board for municipal-wide memos.
[ ] Day 34-35: Training & Career Progression Trackers
    - Seminar/Training logs for personnel development.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEEK 6: SECURITY HARDENING, AUDIT & DEPLOYMENT (Days 36-45)
Focus: Professional Polishing & Launch
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Day 36-38: Full System Audit Logs
    - Non-repudiation logging (Who changed what, where, and when).
    - Log integrity verification (Preventing log tampering).
[ ] Day 39-41: Performance Optimization & Bug Squashing
    - Load testing (Can it handle 500+ employees clocking in simultaneously?).
    - Indexing database queries for sub-second report generation.
[ ] Day 42-43: UAT (User Acceptance Testing)
    - Simulated payroll run with actual HR staff.
    - Feedback loop and minor UI adjustments.
[ ] Day 44-45: Production Deployment & Training
    - Production DB migration.
    - Staff training session & creation of Quick Reference Guides.

================================================================================
TECHNICAL STACK RECOMMENDATION:
Frontend: React 18+ (TS) | Layout: Tailwind CSS | Animation: Framer Motion
Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth + Storage)
Charts: Recharts/D3.js | Date Management: date-fns
